Frequently asked questions

What people ask most before investing.

Do I need permits in Puerto Escondido?

As a demountable modular structure under 60 m², it usually qualifies as minor works / movable property with Urban Development. The municipal fee is calculated on the local UMA. We help with alignment and land use.

What if my land is ejido?

An ideal scenario: mounted on concrete piles with no massive slab, the law protects your home as movable property and your investment is relocatable. Always verify your land’s legal status.

How is the kitchen equipped?

It includes a quartz countertop and an induction cooktop, plus premium interior finishes. Confirm the exact equipment level in your quote.

What costs are NOT included?

The structure price excludes freight, crane handling and foundation (piles/pads). We quote these based on your land and destination.

Is there a warranty?

Yes: a 1-year structural warranty. We provide the conditions in writing before purchase.

Can I pay with crypto?

Yes. We accept XRP, USDT, BTC, ETH and TRX, plus traditional payment, with the price anchored in MXN.

Isn’t it too hot inside?

No. The 75 mm EPS insulated panel blocks the heat; in a real example we visited, a 2-ton mini-split cooled the whole home in ~1 hour and inside you didn’t feel the heat outside.

Won’t it rust near the sea?

The chassis is galvanized steel with marine-grade protection and the exterior materials repel salt. With minimal maintenance (fresh-water rinse, preventive sealing) it holds up very well in coastal climates.

What is its lifespan?

With proper maintenance this type of structure has a long lifespan. We provide the manufacturer’s specs and structural warranty in writing before purchase.

Does it break or damage easily?

It’s an engineered structure, not a rustic container: sandwich panel, galvanized steel and SPC flooring. It comes with a 1-year structural warranty.

Are there repairs or spare parts?

Yes — being modular, parts are replaceable. We support you with after-sales service and any repair it might need.
